## 1. Critical 有判断力的，判断公正（或者审慎）的
A critical reader will notice many errors in this article.

He made a sound critical estimate of the problem.

## 2. Non-fiction 非小说类文学作品
Fiction 小说，虚构
Science fiction 科幻小说
Essay 散文
Novel 小说

## 3. Position 观点，态度，立场
He refused to back away from his position.

The company is in a difficult position now.

## 4. Statement 说明，说法，表态
Make a statement

Soon afterwards she made her first public statement about the affair.

soon afterwards 不久之后

affair 公共事务

## 5. Question 疑问，怀疑
We question if his application will be approved.

I would never question her honesty.

## 6. Evaluate 估计，评价
The professor was asked to evaluate the current situation.

近义词

evaluate 仔细考虑做出评价，特别是有关好坏和d处的评价

estimate 通常指个人做出的主观评价

assess 通过估价以更好利用

rate 专指评定价值等级的高低

## 7. Context 背景，环境，来龙去脉
Can you guess the meaning of the word from the context?

## 8. Value 是非标准，价值观
Present value 现值，面值

Value investing 价值投资，价值投资法

Exchange value 汇兑价值，外汇价值

## 9. Represent 描述，表现
The film intended to represent the lives of ordinary people.

## 10. Assertion 明确肯定，断言
He repeated his assertion that he was not guilty.

repeat 重复

Researchers have recently challenged these assertions.

## 11. Sufficient 足够的，充足的
His income is sufficient for his needs.

The police have sufficient evidence to connect the suspect with the explosion.

evidence 证明，证据

explosion 爆炸

suspect 猜测，嫌疑人

## 12. Statistic 统计数字，统计资料
同：data/figure

according to statistics 据统计

Statistics suggest that the population of this country will be double in ten years'time.

suggest 显示

population 人口

## 13. Integrate 使合并，使成为一体
integrate with 使与...结合

integrate into 成为一体，融入，使...并入

We must integrate theory with practice.

practice 实践

The committee integrated some of the suggestions into its plan.

committee 委员会

## 14. Authority 专家，学术权威
local authority 地方当局，地方政权

work of authorities 权威著作

## 15. Compare 比较
compare A with B

Compare this new TV set with the old one, you will see which is better.

Compared with what we had to go through, his life is paradise already.

paradise 天堂

## 16. Consistent 相符的，符合的
consistent quality 始终如一的质量

consistent policy 一贯的政策

consistent principle 一致性原则

A be consistent with B 符合，与...一致

Her story was not consistent with the facts.

His action is always consistent with his words.

Inconsistent 不一致

## 17. Assumption 假设，假定
His assumption proved to be wrong.

We suspect the assumption that the work will be finished tomorrow.

## 18. Identify 找到，发现
She learned how to identify medicinal herbs from a traditional Chinese doctor.

herbs 草药

Identity 身份

Identification 鉴别

## 19. Valid 符合逻辑的，合理的
valid certificate 有效证书，有效凭证

This train ticket is valid for three days.

## 20. Credible 可靠的，可信的
同：reliable

credible information 可靠的信息

## 21. Landmark 里程碑
Landmark achievement 里程碑式的成就

## 22. Relevant 相关的
I don't think hs remarks are relevant to our discussion.

remark 言语

He consulted a number of relevant books and periodicals.

consult 查阅

periodical （尤指学术）期刊

## 23. Current 当前的
current situation 当前的形势

We try and keep ourselves informed about current trends.

informed 被告知，了解

## 24. Appropriate 恰当的，合适的
同：proper

appropriate time 恰当时间

## 25. Bias 偏见，偏心，偏向
Some people have a bias against foreigners.

The newspaper has a clear bias towards the Democratic Party.

Democratic Party 民主党

## 26. Considerably 相当地，非常地
He lost his way, which delayed him considerably.

delay 耽搁

consider 考虑

consider doing sth 考虑做某事

considerate 体贴的

considerable 大量的

The problem is considerably difficult to us.

## 27. Democrat 民主主义者
A democrat usually gets the union's support.

The president is a Democrat.

## 28. Republican 拥护共和政体的人
Charles is a Republican

## 29. Reflect 反映，反射，反省，归咎，显示
Her sad looks reflected the thought passing through her mind.

looks 外貌

The sunlight was reflected in the water.

## 30. Informed 见多识广的，消息灵通的，有学问的，通知，告诉
Reading is an excellent way to learn new things and stay informed.

The secretary informed me of his arrival.

arrival 抵达
